Questions
What's a credit?
Every AI action — polishing a note, generating flashcards, analyzing an essay — costs a small number of credits based on actual usage. 1 credit ≈ $0.01 of AI processing.
Do unused credits roll over?
No — your credit balance refreshes to your plan's monthly amount every 30 days. This keeps pricing simple and predictable.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Cancel whenever you like and you'll keep your plan's benefits until the end of your current billing period, then drop back to Free.
Will my notes, decks, or essays disappear if I downgrade?
Never. Downgrading only changes future limits — anything you've already created stays exactly as it is.
